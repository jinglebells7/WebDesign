Michael Jordan: The Greatest Player of All Time

Michael Jordan started his career in North Carolina. When he played high school basketball he was cut from the varsity team as a sophomore. This motivated him intensely! After that, he worked on his game endlessly. This drive is what led him to play for North Carolina.

After playing at North Carolina, Michael Jordan was selected by the Chicago Bulls in the 1985 draft with the 3rd overall pick. This again upset Michael. He wanted to be the number 1 overall pick.
